About MTG Workshop

MTG Workshop was built by Nicole Honl using the magicthegathering.io API and Python SDK. As someone who has played magic with her husband for many years, Nicole thought a website where one could build their dream deck and pick out the cards they wished to buy would be helpful. In the future, Nicole would like to add functionality to this website for a Life Counter, recording Game Data, and card market prices. For any questions, concerns, or additional ideas, don't hesitate to reach out to the email below.

As a final note, this website is not produced, endorsed, supported, or affiliated with Wizards of the Coast.
